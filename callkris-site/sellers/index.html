Our Selling Advantage

Our selling process

Stage 1

The discovery process

It all begins here. During our initial consultation we discuss your goals, timeline and expectations for the sale, provide a market analysis, and suggest a listing price. We'll also offer guidance on how to prepare the property to maximize its potential value.

Stage 2

Pre-listing prep

Preparing your property is critical to attracting buyers and achieving the best outcome. We'll work with you to stage your home effectively, make any necessary repairs, and ensure it's clean and decluttered for showings. Need contractors? We've built relationships with dozens.

Stage 3

A personalized marketing plan

Every property is unique and deserves a personalized marketing plan to attract the right buyers and achieve the best price. We build a tailored strategy around your specific goals and the unique features of your home.

Stage 4

Offers and negotiations

When we receive an offer, we help you determine whether it's a good one. We negotiate the terms of the sale — price, closing date, contingencies — and provide guidance on the strength of each offer so you can make informed decisions.

Stage 5

Closing the sale

Once an offer is accepted, we facilitate the closing process — coordinating with lawyers and lenders to ensure a smooth transaction. We review all closing documents with you and address any issues that arise along the way.

Stage 6

Post-sale care

Our relationship doesn't stop at closing. After the sale, we follow up to ensure your satisfaction and address any outstanding issues. We're committed to supporting you throughout the selling process and beyond.

Home Seller's FAQ

Most frequently asked questions

How do I determine the value of my home?

We help you determine the value of your home with a comparative market analysis — reviewing recent sales of similar properties, active listings, and those cancelled or terminated in your area, while assessing current market conditions.

What should I do to prepare my home for sale?

We recommend decluttering and depersonalizing, making any necessary repairs, and staging to showcase your home's best features. Think bright, open and airy — neutral colours with a simple accent are always a good bet. We use a mix of traditional and virtual staging and will guide you on how to make your home more appealing to buyers.

How do I find a real estate agent to sell my home?

Ask around — real estate is personal, and chances are your friends and family know someone they've worked with. Make sure they're experienced, check reviews, and ask questions. Request samples of marketing materials and a detailed valuation report. Do they use lockboxes? Are they there for showings? How many active and sold listings do they have?

We'd love the opportunity to show you what we've got. Click, call or text and we'll share our marketing plan, discuss our experience, and answer any questions you have.

How long will it take to sell my home?

It varies with market conditions and other factors, but on average our listings sell in just 17 days. We'll provide expert guidance and keep you informed throughout the entire process.

How should I price my home?

Strategically — and we'll show you how. We perform a comprehensive analysis of your home's value and suggest a price range appropriate for your property and objectives, taking into account current market conditions and our decades of experience.

What should I expect during the home selling process?

Expect plenty of communication to get things right. We'll help you navigate the entire process, from preparing your home for sale to closing the deal, providing expert guidance every step of the way so you can make informed decisions.

What are my obligations as a seller?

You'll need to be willing to show your home, provide full disclosure about its condition, and work with the buyer to ensure a smooth transaction. You should be willing and able to sell at the agreed-upon list price. We'll guide you through your obligations and the legal requirements at each stage.

How do I negotiate with buyers?

We negotiate on your behalf to help you get the best possible outcome, providing expert advice on the strengths and weaknesses of each offer and helping you determine the appropriate counteroffer.

What are the costs associated with selling a home?

Our fees are 7% on the first $100,000 + 2.5% on the balance. We recommend budgeting around $1,250 for lawyer or notary fees. If you're also purchasing, keep in mind Property Transfer Tax: 1% on the first $200,000, 2% on the portion from $200,000–$2,000,000, and 3% on the portion above $2,000,000. Other costs can include moving, staging, additional closing costs and repairs. We'll provide a detailed estimate of the costs you can expect.

Should I make repairs or renovations before selling?

A picture-perfect home leaves little doubt on the table. Of course, life isn't always perfect — and we pride ourselves on bringing listings to life no matter what they look like. On request, we'll advise on the repairs and renovations likely to have the greatest impact on your sale price.
